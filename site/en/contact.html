Our Story

Precision engineering, unwavering commitment.

IMB Brasil was born from the need for equipment that could withstand the rigor of Brazilian climate and terrain. What started as a small maintenance shop became one of the country's leading paver and extruder manufacturers.

Our journey is marked by continuous innovation. Every part leaving our factory passes quality control aligned with the most demanding international standards. We don't just deliver machines — we deliver the foundation for entire cities' progress.
